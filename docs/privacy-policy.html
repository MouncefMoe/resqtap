Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

ResqTap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and email address for authentication purposes.
  • Favorites: Emergency guides you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Basic device type for app compatibility.
  • Usage Data: Which emergency guides are viewed (for improving content).
Important: ResqTap does NOT collect any personal health information, medical records, or sensitive personal data. All emergency guide content is educational and for informational purposes only.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the emergency guide service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To save your favorite emergency guides
  • To improve our app and emergency content
  • To send important service updates (if you opt-in)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored as follows:

  • Local Storage: Favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Account credentials are securely managed through AWS Cognito
  • Emergency Content: Loaded from our secure AWS servers

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AWS Cognito: For secure user authentication
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): For hosting our backend services

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your data from our systems.

Local data (favorites, preferences) remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear the app data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

ResqTap is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Medical Disclaimer

ResqTap provides emergency first aid information for educational purposes only. This app is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of qualified health providers with any questions regarding medical conditions. In case of emergency, always call your local emergency services (911 in the US).
